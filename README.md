## The Bet (M1)

**What we're building, for whom, why now.**

* **Product:** AI Lifestyle Companion
* **AI Value Archetype:** Copilot
* **Vulnerability Scores:** Moat 3/5 · Data 4/5 · Platform 2/5
* **Top Risk:** The largest vulnerability is platform encroachment. Major ecosystem players already own health data, wearable integrations, and user attention. If Apple, Google, or OpenAI launch a deeply integrated AI Lifestyle Companion, they could replicate many core features quickly.
* **Confidence:** M
* **Prototype:** https://ai-lifestyle-companion.lovable.app
* **Kill Criteria:** We will stop pursuing this concept if user testing shows that:

  * Users do not perceive sufficient value beyond existing health and fitness apps.
  * Users are unwilling to share lifestyle and wellbeing data with an AI companion.
  * Engagement drops below 25% weekly active usage after 90 days.
  * Personalized recommendations fail to improve measurable wellbeing outcomes.

## The Moat (M2)

**Why this won't get copied in 6 months.**

* **Data Flywheel Score:** 4/5

* **Encroachment Defense:** Build a coach-and-provider ecosystem where aggregated outcomes improve intervention quality across users, coaches, and healthcare partners. Focus on behavioral change data and long-term habit outcomes rather than generic AI conversations.
* **Vendor Portability:** Partial

## The Contract (M4)

**Why users will trust a probabilistic system.**

* **Reliability Target:** ≥85%
* **Golden Dataset:** Evidence-based lifestyle recommendations validated against nutrition, exercise, sleep, and behavioral health guidelines.
* **Confidence UX:** Display recommendation confidence levels, explain rationale, and clearly distinguish guidance from medical advice.
* **HITL Architecture:** Human Review Trigger for mental health risks, self-harm indicators, eating disorder signals, or persistent wellbeing decline.
* **Failure Mode Coverage:** Misinterpretation of user context, overconfidence in recommendations, and unsafe health guidance monitored through continuous evaluation.

## The Guardrails (M5)

**What breaks when this scales — and what compounds.**

* **Compounding System:** Personalized behavioral models improve as users complete activities, provide feedback, and achieve outcomes.
* **Governance Posture:** Human-supervised AI with transparent decision-making and privacy-by-design principles.
* **Autonomy Boundaries:** AI recommends and coaches but never diagnoses, prescribes, or overrides healthcare professionals.
* **Escalation Triggers:** Mental health concerns, signs of burnout, high-risk behaviors, or repeated negative wellbeing trends.
* **Audit Cadence:** Quarterly model review and monthly safety monitoring.
* **Shadow AI Audit (user-side):** Monitor user reliance patterns and identify over-dependence on AI-generated guidance.
* **Agent Boundaries:** Coaching Agent provides motivation, planning, reflection, and accountability only.
* **Regulatory Exposure:** Medium — GDPR, AI Act compliance, and potential wellness-to-medical classification risks.

## The Pitch (M6)

**How you get this funded, shipped, and adopted.**

* **Horizon 1 (Now):** Launch a consumer AI lifestyle coach focused on daily wellbeing, habit formation, and engagement.

* **Horizon 2 (Next):** Integrate wearables, health platforms, and professional coaching services to create a personalized wellbeing ecosystem.

* **Horizon 3 (Bet):** Become the trusted AI wellbeing layer connecting individuals, employers, coaches, and healthcare providers around preventative health outcomes.

* **Board Narrative:**
  **The case:** The wellbeing market is saturated with trackers and dashboards but underserved in personalized action and accountability. AI enables scalable, individualized coaching that was previously only available through expensive human services. The company can own the behavioral layer between health data and meaningful lifestyle change.

* **Ask:**

  * Funding for product development and AI infrastructure.
  * Investment in user acquisition and retention experiments.
  * Strategic partnerships with coaches, employers, and wellbeing providers.
  * 18-month runway to validate engagement, retention, and health outcome improvements.

* **Key Strategic Change:** Shift from a health-tracking application to an AI-powered behavior change platform that creates measurable wellbeing outcomes through continuous coaching and personalization.
